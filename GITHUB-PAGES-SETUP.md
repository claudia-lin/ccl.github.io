# Turn on your GitHub site (GitHub Pages)

Your sunglasses try-on app is in this repo. To make it live on the web:

## Steps

1. Open your repo in the browser: **https://github.com/claudia-lin/ccl.github.io**
2. Click **Settings** (top right of the repo).
3. In the left sidebar, click **Pages** (under "Code and automation").
4. Under **Build and deployment**:
   - **Source:** choose **Deploy from a branch**
   - **Branch:** select **main**
   - **Folder:** select **/ (root)**
5. Click **Save**.

After 1–2 minutes, your site will be at:

**https://claudia-lin.github.io/ccl.github.io/**

(You’ll also see this URL at the top of the Pages settings.)

---

**If your repo is named exactly `claudia-lin.github.io`** (user site), the URL will be:

**https://claudia-lin.github.io/**
